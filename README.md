# 🚀 Crypto Super Screener

Crypto Super Screener is a high-performance crypto market analysis tool designed to give traders speed, clarity, and control. Whether you're actively trading or just exploring opportunities, this platform helps you cut through the noise and focus on what matters.

---

## 🔥 Features

### • Full Market Dashboard
Access a unified view of the crypto market using CCXT integrations across multiple exchanges.

### • Real-Time Filtering
Quickly identify assets that match your strategy — from high volatility trades to stable setups.

### • Multi-Chart Grid
Monitor multiple charts at once with a clean, customizable layout. No more tab switching.

### • High Performance
Fast refresh rates ensure you stay aligned with live market conditions.

### • Actionable Data
Structured analytics and clean visuals help support smarter trading decisions.
